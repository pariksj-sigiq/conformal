EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

From diagnostic to deployed AI
THESIS

Partner with SFS to design and deploy AI across value chain – from a CEO decision cockpit to vernacular
farmer–facing agents – through a unified platform of AI Copilots, Autopilots, and Console that operates them.
DIAGNOSTIC

Project Leap surfaced 215 use cases across 12 solutions; 12 hero priorities for Wave 1 (0–12 months) – spanning
farmer engagement, channel-partner workflows, manufacturing, R&D, andan enterprise CEO cockpit. SFS sits at
digital maturity of 2.1/5 today (integration 1.4, analytics 1.0); foundation needs to be built in parallel with use cases.
APPROACH

Conformal embeds with SFS teams to build, ship, and operate use cases – Three layers of AI tech (Copilots,
Autopilots, Conformal Console) delivered through a Diagnose & Design → Build & Run → Drive & Expand lifecycle,
with forward–deployed engineering on the ground.
OUTCOME

Decision velocity for executives. Productivity uplift for field, channel partner, and farmer–facing teams.
Vernacular engagement at scale. Margin and working capital gains across procurement, manufacturing, and finance
– quantified by Bain at INR 50–80 Cr topline (field force) and INR 10–15 Cr savings (procurement) within 2 years.
TEAM

Builder leadership combining enterprise AI scale (Amazon, Crescendo), large–scale operating transformation
(BCG), and India SME capital experience (Accel). BITS Pilani · Wharton.
