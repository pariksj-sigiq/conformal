## Current story

The recommended SFS-facing story is:

1. SFS gave Conformal use-case narratives, not a confirmed field-level schema.
2. Conformal decomposed the 15 custom-build use cases into 146 assumed data fields.
3. 33 are likely available, 19 are partial and 94 are unavailable.
4. The 94 unavailable fields are not 94 blockers: 58 are computed by Conformal, 27 are externally sourced and roughly 9 need SFS workflow/governance decisions.
5. The first ask is masked extracts, schemas and source owners, not production credentials.
6. Wave 1 should validate Enterprise Assistant, Field Performance and Dealer Profiles while building the shared data foundation.
